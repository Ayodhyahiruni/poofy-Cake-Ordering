Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing orders at least 48 hours in advance for standard cakes. Custom designs may require 3-5 days notice.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer local delivery within 10 miles for $10. Pickup is always free and available by appointment.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can create gluten-free, sugar-free, and vegan options. Please mention your requirements when ordering.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and digital payments. A 50% deposit is required for custom orders over $100.

How should I store my cake?

Keep your cake refrigerated and bring to room temperature 30 minutes before serving. Best enjoyed within 2-3 days.

Do you provide cake cutting services?

For special events, we can provide cake cutting and serving assistance. Please ask about this service when ordering.
